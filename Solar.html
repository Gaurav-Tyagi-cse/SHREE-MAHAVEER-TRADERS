Solar Light

A solar light is a lighting system that uses solar energy to produce electricity, which is then stored in batteries and used to emit light:

Solar lights are made up of a solar rechargeable battery, an LED light, and a photoresistor that detects low light. During the day, the solar cells in the light gather energy from sunlight and charge the battery. When it's dark, the light turns on automatically, and when it's light outside, the light turns off.

Solar lights are a sustainable and cost-effective lighting option that can be used in many settings, including homes, businesses, and public spaces. They can be used for a variety of purposes, such as: Pathway lights, Wall-mounted lamps, Freestanding lamp posts, Security lights, and Patio lights.
